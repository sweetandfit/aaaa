Nutrition facts

Ingredients: dried dates, dark chocolate (90 % cocoa), organic coconut flour, organic coconut oil, peanut butter, hazelnuts, maple syrup.
Contains: tree nuts, peanuts

calories: 85 kcal

Nutrition information provided for 1 praline with the weight of approximately 17 g.

total carbohydrate: 7,9 g

Carbs are the source of your energy, don't be afraid of them! Just pay attention to WHAT carbs you choose. Try to balance your diet to give your body the right amount of "fuel" carbs.

total fat: 5,7 g

You will get just the good fats here! All organic, all natural! You will NOT find here any hydrogenated fats! Just the coconut oil and valuable fats from nuts.

sugars: 5,2 g

Yes, pralines contain some sweetness. But wach now - there is no white sugar inside! All sugars in our pralines come from maple syrup and dates - that's it! Healthy alternative!

protein: 1,1 g

Protein is an important building block of bones, muscles, cartilage, skin, blood and every cell! Your body uses protein to build and repair tissues. You also use protein to make enzymes, hormones, and other body chemicals.

dietary fiber: 1,7 g

Fiber is the indigestible part of plant foods that travels through our digestive system, absorbing water along the way and easing bowel movements! Try to keep it in your everyday diet to stay healthy and fit!
